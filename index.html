Weekend Route
Humboldthain to Weinberg Park
Start in Volkspark Humboldthain — grab a coffee, check out the rose garden or climb the flak tower. Then wander through Mauerpark for a snack and the Sunday flea market. Finish at Volkspark am Weinberg on the grassy hill under (hopefully) clear skies.
~3 km · ~40 min · Best on Sundays
Find Gardens
Green Space
Your Local Community Garden
Berlin has a handful of community gardens where you can rent a plot and grow your own vegetables and flowers. Many host open garden days, workshops on woodworking and beekeeping, and more. Find the closest one and go check it out — it might become your next favorite hobby.
Various locations across Berlin

Classic Wander
Tiergarten
Pick any entrance and start walking. No Google Maps. Follow whichever paths look interesting — maybe you'll end up at the English Rose Garden, Café am Neuen See, or the Soviet War Memorial. Pack snacks, a thermos of coffee, and a camera.
~3 km long · ~1 km wide

Day Trip
Racecourse Hoppegarten
The largest racetrack in Germany is just an hour outside Berlin. With 145 years of horse racing history, it's the perfect excuse to escape the city. Even if you skip the races, the surrounding areas are worth a stroll.
~1 hr from Berlin · Tickets from 14€
